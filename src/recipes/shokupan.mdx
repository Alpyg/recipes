## Ingredients

---

### Yudane
- **60 g** High protein flour
- **50 g** Boiled water

---

### Shokupan
- **250 g** High protein flour
- **4 g** Yeast
- **3 g** Salt
- **35 g** Sugar
- **1 tbsp** Milk powder
- **30 g** Egg (1 egg, beaten)
- **150 ml** Milk
- **30 g** Unsalted butter (room temp)

</Card>



<Card>

## Steps

---

### Yudane
- Mix boiling water with flour well and rest for **3 hours** or **overnight**

---

### Shokupan
- Mix dry ingredients
- Add egg and Yudane
- Add milk slowly while mixing
- Add butter and mix again
- Rest for **30 min**
- Split dough, reknead and rest for **15 min**
- Flatten, fold, roll into shape and let rest in mold for **15 min**

</Card>
